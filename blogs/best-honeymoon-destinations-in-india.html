Some of the tempting attractions that allure great number of visitors especially the honeymooners to Manali are Vashisth Temple, Tibetan Monastaries, Rohtang Pass, Jagatsukh, Arjun Gufa, etc. Beside these if you love adventure and thrill, Manali is the ideal destination for you.

    3. Kerala - The Land of God’s Own Country

    Kerala is a wonderful state and is one of the highly preferred destinations visited by tourists round the year. Gifted with amazing and unique natural beauty and tempting sightseeing spots, Kerala has gained a world fame for its astonishing beauty especially for its tempting natural splendour. Its charming backwaters, cool and refreshing hill stations, tranquil silver beaches, profound greenery and more of all the world class hospitality, all make Kerala an ideal gateway to enjoy romantic and enjoyable honeymoon holidays. The charming natural beauty, green hills, view of the lofty Himalayas, picturesque lakes, verdant green forest and more of all the enchanting natural beauty all make Sikkim an ideal destination to enjoy vacation with an ultimate experience. Sikkim is truly the perfect destination where couples can enjoy romantic honeymoon holidays in the blissful ambiance of the nature.
