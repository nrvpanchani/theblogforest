Most Romantic Honeymoon Destination in The World

Posted On : December 2019

Nirav Panchani

159

Romantic honeymoon is dream for every couple for newly couple best honeymoon destinations to travel in the world on 2020 beautiful honeymoon destinations to experience most romantic honeymoon.

1. Maldives

Honeymoon Style: Luxury

A Maldives honeymoon is incomplete without experiencing a stay at a private beach resort near the beautiful beaches in the Maldives. When thinking of Maldives, the first thing that comes to mind are the huts suspended over the azure water of the Indian ocean that guarantee luxury and an epic time spent enjoying the salubrious weather of Maldives. Imagine laying on the soft white sands with your partner, taking a dip in the azure-colored waters while completely alone on an entire island.

Romantic Things to Do:

A Candlelit Dinner on a Private Island.

An Exotic Couples SPA Therapy

Underwater Dining

View Marine Life

Take Cruise Ride

Fishing

2. Bali

Honeymoon Style: Culture/Adventure

Bali is an Indonesian island known for its forested volcanic mountains, iconic rice paddies, beaches and coral reefs. Bali is surrounded by water from 90% of the sides, making it a perfect destination of a honeymoon. The Beaches are ultimate, blue waters are regular sites, bekateaches are also famous for surfing.

Romantic Things to Do:

Go Horseback Riding

Eat a Sunset Dinner

Go on Night Cruise

Enjoy a Helicopter Ride.

Go Scuba Diving or Snorkeling

Party in Kuta & Seminyak

3. Mauritius

Honeymoon Style: Relaxing

A dream destination for honeymooners, Mauritius is a fantastic place where you can relish the adventures and rejoice with the nature. A favourite in the honeymoon destination list, Mauritius is tucked away amidst the Mascarene Islands. Mauritius is a slice of paradise whose very name evokes images of azure blue seas and pristine beaches.

Romantic Things to Do:

Beach-Hopping

Ile Aux Cerfs

Take a private cruise to watch a spectacular Mauritius sunset

Relish Mauritian Spas

Pamplemousses Botanical Garden

Grand Bassin

4. Phuket and Krabi

Honeymoon Style: Fun/Party

When you think Thailand, you probably think bachelor/ette parties but Phuket is, undoubtedly, a romantic destination. Its sandy beaches & beautiful waters come together to give a picture postcard like appeal to the destination..

Romantic Things to Do:

Have a Romantic Dinner by the Sea

Stay in a Private Pool Villa!

Enjoy a cocktail from a rooftop sunset bar

Get a Romance in Phang Nga Bay

Sleep on an Island

5. Lucerne and Paris

Honeymoon Style: Culture/Classic Romance

A honeymoon in Paris: it’s hard to imagine anything more romantic than that! If years of Bollywood have taught us anything, it’s that no true love is complete without a trip to Switzerland.Celebrate your love with a trip to Switzerland

Romantic Things to Do:

Watch the sunset (or the sunrise) at the Eiffel Tower

Take in amazing views over Paris

Admire beautiful houses of worship

Spend a day escaping to Versailles

Take in a classic movie
